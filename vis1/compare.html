This page provides users with the ability to compare two different regions over the 13 available data variables. You may choose to compare them either as a line or a bar chart and they are plotted on two seperate chart areas. To start off, please select the chart tyoe for the first chart.

*Please note that if no graph is plotted in the chart area then values for that particular variable is unavailable forthe selected country/region. Please try selecting a different region
or variable to proceed with your exploration.
